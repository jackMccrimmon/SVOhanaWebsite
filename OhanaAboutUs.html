About the Crew

Hey there, we are Tim and Ann from North Carolina! We are fairly new to sailing having recently purchased a 2009 Jeanneau Sun Odyssey 42 Deck Salon named Sailing Vessel Ohana. We have been married for 31 years and have 2 boys, Patrick and Jack. Patrick is in the military and Jack is finishing his undergrad studies in Computer Science. For now, we are continuing to work full time and spend as much free time as possible sailing the Atlantic coast around Oriental NC. When we retire, we would like to do the live-aboard thing and travel wherever the wind blows us.

Tim McCrimmon
